Harnessing AI to predict customer behavior in banking.

About This Project

This application is powered by a Random Forest model trained on the Bank Marketing Dataset. The dataset includes details about customers and their interactions with a financial institution, allowing us to predict whether a client will agree to make a fixed-term deposit.

By leveraging Python, Flask, and machine learning, I personally trained the model to provide predictions based on customer profiles.

Need assistance? The Help button is available in the Deposit Approval Predictor module. Use it to understand the app's features and variable explanations.
